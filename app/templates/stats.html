How these numbers are calculated

Every figure here is measured, not estimated. Each verification logs the API's reported token usage, and the dollar cost is computed from the model's published price:

cost = input_tokens / 1,000,000 × input_price + output_tokens / 1,000,000 × output_price

The price table is dated and sourced in app/costs.py (PRICING). For Claude Haiku 4.5 that is $1.00 per 1M input tokens and $5.00 per 1M output tokens.

To audit any figure, read metrics/usage.jsonl (one JSON record per call, with the token counts, cost, and verdict) and cross-check a record against the price table. Nothing is back-estimated.
